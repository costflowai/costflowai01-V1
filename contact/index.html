Frequently Asked Questions

Are the calculators free to use?

Yes! All our core calculator functionality is completely free. We may offer premium features in the future, but the essential tools will always be available at no cost.

How accurate are the cost estimates?

Our calculators use industry-standard formulas and real-world data. However, actual costs can vary based on local market conditions, specific materials chosen, and project complexity. Always verify with local suppliers.

Can I save my calculations?

Currently, calculations are saved locally in your browser. We're working on user accounts and cloud storage for future releases.

Do you have an API?

We're developing an API for developers and businesses. Contact us at business@costflowai.com for early access information.
