Concrete Estimation Guide

Accurate concrete estimation is critical for project success. This guide covers the fundamentals of calculating concrete quantities for slabs, foundations, and other structural elements.

Basic Calculation

The basic formula for concrete volume is:

  • Volume (cubic feet) = Length × Width × Thickness
  • Volume (cubic yards) = Volume (cubic feet) ÷ 27

Key Considerations

  • Add 5-10% waste factor
  • Account for subgrade preparation
  • Consider reinforcement requirements
  • Factor in delivery logistics

ROM Limitations

Important: These are ROM (Rough Order of Magnitude) estimates. Always validate with:

  • Licensed structural engineers
  • Local suppliers for current pricing
  • Site-specific conditions assessment
