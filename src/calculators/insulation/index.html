About This Calculator

How It Works

This calculator determines insulation requirements by calculating material volume needed to achieve target R-value, considering cavity depth and insulation type. Includes coverage rates and installation factors.

Assumptions

  • Standard framing with 16" or 24" centers
  • R-values based on manufacturer specifications
  • Does not account for thermal bridging
  • Pricing based on regional averages
  • Installation by qualified professionals

Professional Use

This calculator provides ROM estimates for energy code compliance and cost planning. Actual R-values may vary based on installation quality and environmental conditions. Consult local energy codes for requirements.
