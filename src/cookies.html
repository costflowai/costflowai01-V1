Cookie Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to remember preferences and improve your experience.

Cookies We Use

  • Essential: Required for site functionality
  • Analytics: Help us understand usage (anonymized)
  • Preferences: Remember your calculator settings

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies

Impact of Disabling

Disabling cookies may affect:

  • Calculator functionality
  • Preference saving
  • Site analytics
