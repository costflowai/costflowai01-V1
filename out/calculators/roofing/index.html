Roofing Calculator

Calculate roofing materials and costs for your project. Get accurate estimates for asphalt, metal, tile, slate, and rubber roofing systems.

Calculation Method

Base Area = Length × Width
Roof Area = Base Area × Pitch Factor
Total Area = Roof Area × 1.15 (waste factor)
Includes materials, underlayment, flashing, and labor

* Estimates are based on standard industry calculations. Actual costs may vary based on location, material quality, and local market conditions. Always consult with professionals for precise project estimation.
