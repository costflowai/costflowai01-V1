Insulation Calculator

Calculate insulation materials and costs for walls and ceilings. Get accurate estimates for fiberglass, cellulose, spray foam, and mineral wool insulation.

Calculation Details

Wall Area = (Length + Width) × 2 × Height × 0.85 (door/window reduction)
Ceiling Area = Length × Width
Includes vapor barrier and installation costs
Energy savings are estimated based on improved thermal performance

* Estimates are based on standard industry calculations. Actual costs may vary based on location, material quality, and local market conditions. Always consult with professionals for precise project estimation.
