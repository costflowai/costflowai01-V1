About CostFlowAI

Professional construction cost estimation tools built for accuracy and reliability

Our Mission

CostFlowAI was created to solve a critical problem in the construction industry: inaccurate cost estimates. Our platform provides professional-grade calculators that help contractors, builders, and project managers create precise, detailed cost estimates for construction projects of all sizes.

What We Offer

  • 22+ Specialized Calculators - From concrete and framing to electrical and HVAC
  • Regional Pricing Data - Accurate material and labor costs for your location
  • Professional Reports - Export to PDF, Excel, and CSV formats
  • Mobile Responsive - Access from any device, anywhere
  • Always Free - Core functionality available at no cost

Built for Professionals

Our calculators are designed by construction professionals for construction professionals. Each tool includes industry-standard calculations, waste factors, and regional pricing adjustments to ensure your estimates are as accurate as possible.
